Packaging Bottle Caps

Caps, like hair, are the crowning glory of a bottle.

Choosing the best cap for your product ensures that your customers find it easy to dispense and use your products.

Proper fitting caps will ensure that your product is protected from excess air exposure and leaking. To learn more about getting properly matching caps and bottles, checkout our Packaging 101 education section or speak with your account manager.

Your account manager will be there to help you understand the various capping options.
