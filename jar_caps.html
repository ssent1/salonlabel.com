Packaging Jar Caps

Caps complete the look, feel, and style of a jar.

Choosing the best cap for your product ensures that your customers find it easy to dispense and use your products.

Square profile caps are best for cap labels.

Proper fitting caps will ensure that your product is protected from excess air exposure and leaking. To learn more about getting properly matching caps and bottles, checkout our Packaging 101 education section.
